3D printing and the environment

This page aims to collect information on how to make sure you handle correctly your 3D printer waste.

If you can, please email your local recycling company and ask them how to dispose of your 3D printer waste. Report in our Telegram group and/or send a pull-request to the wiki.

Important note

Always check with your recycling company before disposing of 3D printer waste. Recycling plants are different and they use different technologies.

Energy

Even if you can afford a higher electricity bill, using more energy than needed is totally unnecessary and avoidable.
